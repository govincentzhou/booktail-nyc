Hidden Gem of NYC

A Cozy Haven for Book, Cat & Coffee Lovers

Tucked away in a quiet alley, Booktail opens up into a lush, overgrown sanctuary. Escape the hustle of the city, cross our koi pond footbridge, and find a cozy nook with a warm cup of coffee and a purring cat.

The Booktail Experience

Take a virtual stroll through our space. Booktail is designed to be an organic, tranquil oasis where literature, cafe culture, and animal companionship meet.

The Ambience

A Lush, Overgrown Oasis

Upon stepping through our alleyway entrance, you'll be greeted by a surprisingly expansive space filled with wood and stone detailing. Warm ambient light hangs from rafters wrapped in ivy, and at the center of it all is a tiny indoor koi pond spanned by a miniature wooden footbridge.

The gentle babbling of the water combined with soft acoustic tunes sets the ultimate relaxing backdrop for reading, studying, or socializing.

Our Bookstore

Curated Shelves & Reading Nooks

Books are placed decoratively yet functionally all around the cafe. We stock a wide, handpicked collection including literary classics, sci-fi & fantasy, thrillers, dramas, non-fiction, and manga.

Settle into any of our comfortable velvet armchairs and read any book in our inventory for free. If you find a story you can't put down, you can easily rent it at the counter or reserve it online to take home.

The Feline Residents

Purring Reading Companions

What's a cozy nook without a cat? Booktail is home to several friendly, rescue cats who roam the space freely. Whether they are sunbathing on bookshelves, curled up in a corner, or demanding head scratches, our cats bring a unique warmth to the bookstore.

We work closely with local shelters to ensure all cats are healthy, happy, and living in an enriched environment that they can call their home.
