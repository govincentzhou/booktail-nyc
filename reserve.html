Cozy Sanctuary

Reserve Your Reading Spot

Due to the popular presence of our resident cats and limited cozy alcoves, we highly recommend making a reservation in advance. Settle in for a dedicated 2-hour window of uninterrupted reading, studying, or socializing.

Reservation Fee

Table reservations are completely free. You only pay for food, drink, or books you rent.

Cat Lounge Experience

Optional cat lounge seating includes access to our primary play and cuddle zones. A $5/guest contribution goes directly to the rescue shelter for food and healthcare.

Grace Period

We hold tables for up to 15 minutes. Please let us know if you are running late!
